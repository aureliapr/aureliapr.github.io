Critical Pedagogy in CS Education

Study Motivation

Why explore the impacts of critical pedagogy in computer science education?

Technology has perpetuated the matrix of domination. It has traditionally served one type of user. There are well documented instances of technological design failing to accomodate different types of users. An example of this is soap dispesers that only recognize pale skin tones. Changing the way society approaches technological design starts in undergraduate education. By encorperating ethical thinking into computer science education we can instill a sense of responsibility in upcoming software engineers and designers.

Previous Studies

What were the limitations of previous studies?

Professors are integrating critical pedagogy into undergraduate computer science education. They have found that the intergration of ethics into a final integrative class at the end of university is not effective. However, integration throughout students' college education is more effective. Previous research provided qualitative evidence for how effective the critical pedagogy was.

Valuing Marginalized Perspectives From ___ to ____

Throughout the course, students developed their understanding of how to include marginalized perspectives in the design of technology. At the start of the course, they had a general idea of the importance of marginalized perspectives. However, it was not until after software design that they developed concrete strategies to accommodate marginalized groups of people.
